✦ Process

How We Score

01
🔍

Research & Source

We aggregate data from ESG reports, government filings, investigative journalism, NGO assessments, and academic research across 14 industry verticals.

02
⚖️

Score & Weight

Each dimension is scored 0–100, weighted by its ethical impact. Community members vote on weightings yearly — making our methodology democratically accountable.

03
📣

Publish & Pressure

Scores are published openly. We send reports directly to company boards, partner with journalists, and run targeted campaigns to create real accountability.

✦ Our Framework

What We Measure

🌾

Supply Chain Ethics

Labor practices, child labor risk, fair trade certifications, sourcing transparency, and supplier audits throughout the full production chain.

WEIGHT: 20%

👷

Worker Wellbeing

Living wages, benefits, union rights, safety records, worker satisfaction surveys, CEO pay ratio, and anti-discrimination practices.

WEIGHT: 18%

🌍

Sustainability

Carbon emissions, net-zero commitments, packaging waste, water usage, biodiversity impact, and credibility of environmental pledges.

WEIGHT: 18%

🤝

Diversity & Inclusion

Board and leadership diversity, pay equity audits, LGBTQ+ policies, accessibility, community investment, and inclusive hiring practices.

WEIGHT: 14%

🗳️

Political Alignment

Political donations, lobbying activity, policy advocacy stances, voter suppression risk, and alignment with democratic institutions and human rights.

WEIGHT: 14%

💰

Value vs. Quality

Honest pricing, ingredient/product quality, shrinkflation tracking, hidden fees, and whether cost-cutting comes at customer or worker expense.

WEIGHT: 10%

😊

Customer Experience

Data privacy practices, customer service quality, dark patterns, refund/return policies, and whether customers are treated with dignity.

WEIGHT: 8%

🏘️

Community Impact

Tax practices, local business displacement, community investment, philanthropic track record, and net impact on neighborhoods where they operate.

WEIGHT: 8%

✦ Our Mission
